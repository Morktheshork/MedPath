How It Works

A transparent, structured process designed to ensure the right fit and set clear expectations from the start.

1

Schedule Free Consultation

Begin with a complimentary 60-minute onboarding call. We'll discuss your academic goals, current preparation status, and specific needs to determine if our approach aligns with your objectives.

2

Fit Assessment

During the consultation, we evaluate whether our tutoring style and methodology match your learning preferences and goals. If we're a good fit, we proceed. If not, we'll suggest alternative resources better suited to your needs.

Customized Study Plan

Schedule one-on-one tutoring sessions at times that work for you. We maintain regular progress check-ins, adjust strategies as needed, and provide ongoing financial planning support.

Terms & Policies

Clear expectations and professional standards that ensure fairness and respect for all parties.

Payment Terms

Rate: Services are provided at a flat rate of $100.00 per hour.

Billing: Clients are billed after each completed session. Payment is due within thirty (30) calendar days of the session date.

Accepted Payment Methods

  • Venmo
  • PayPal
  • Zelle

Late Payment: Failure to remit payment within the thirty-day period may result in suspension of future booking privileges until the outstanding balance is resolved.

Scheduling & Availability

Session Duration: All tutoring sessions are scheduled in one-hour increments.

Standard Availability

Sessions are typically available during the following hours:

  • Monday through Friday: 5:00 PM - 9:00 PM
  • Weekend sessions: Available on a limited basis depending on tutor availability

Scheduling Authority: All scheduling is managed by the Head Tutor, who maintains authority over availability, time slot allocation, and scheduling modifications. Clients should direct all scheduling requests through the designated booking system or directly to the Head Tutor.

Cancellation Policy

We understand that scheduling conflicts arise and accommodate reasonable cancellation requests.

Standard Cancellations: Clients may cancel scheduled sessions without penalty, provided notice is given.

Repeated Cancellations: After three (3) consecutive cancellations by the same client, a cancellation fee may be assessed at the discretion of the service provider. This policy is in place to ensure fair access to limited appointment slots and to respect tutor availability.

We encourage clients to reschedule rather than cancel whenever possible to maintain continuity in preparation.

No-Show Policy

A "no-show" is defined as failure to attend a scheduled session without providing prior notice to the tutor.

Enforcement: No-show occurrences are not tolerated, as they represent a significant disruption to scheduling and tutor time management.

Consequences: Two (2) no-show occurrences will result in immediate termination of the client relationship and discontinuation of all services. No exceptions will be made to this policy.

This policy ensures fairness to all clients and maintains the professional standards necessary for effective tutoring services.

Service Discretion

MedPath reserves the right to discontinue services to any client if policies outlined in this document are repeatedly violated, if professional boundaries are not respected, or if the tutoring relationship is deemed no longer productive.

All decisions regarding service continuation are made at the sole discretion of MedPath and are intended to maintain a professional, respectful environment conducive to student success.

Onboarding & Fit Assessment

Initial Consultation: All prospective clients are offered a complimentary 60-minute onboarding consultation to assess needs, goals, and mutual fit.

Fit Determination: Following the consultation, MedPath will determine whether the client's needs align with our tutoring methodology and approach. If deemed a good fit, the client will be invited to schedule paid sessions. If not a suitable match, the client will be informed respectfully and, when appropriate, referred to alternative resources or services better suited to their needs.

This process protects both client investment and tutor resources by ensuring alignment before any financial commitment is made.
